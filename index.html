About

As a product designer and design leader, I craft innovative and resonant experiences that inspire engagement and loyalty in users and deliver measurable value to businesses. I take a multidisciplinary and collaborative approach to shaping and shipping cogent, data-rich, and often award-winning interaction experiences through strategy, research, and design.
